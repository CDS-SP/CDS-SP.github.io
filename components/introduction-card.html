Why It Works.


As Jesse Livermore once said, "The market is never wrong, but opinions often are."

Our indicator strips away bias by objectively analyzing price action. By comparing each candlestick to the one four periods prior, it identifies persistent upward or downward momentum. When this sequence repeats, it signals an imbalance of market pressure , where buyers or sellers continuously absorb liquidity until their force is depleted. This progressive exhaustion of market participation increases the probability of a reversal, allowing you to anticipate turning points with quantifiable precision rather than gut feeling.
